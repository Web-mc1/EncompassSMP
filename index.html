About

EncompassSMP combines scripted storytelling with free-range survival gameplay. While the server follows an overarching narrative centered around four key characters, players are free to build, explore, form alliances, create rivalries, and shape the world in their own way.

The Story

EncompassSMP is a world filled with mysteries, ancient secrets, forgotten conflicts, and powerful forces hidden beneath the surface. While four central characters drive the main narrative, every player has the opportunity to influence the future of the world through their actions, alliances, discoveries, and rivalries.
